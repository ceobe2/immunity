What is vaccine?

A vaccine is a type of medicine that trains the body’s immune system so that it can fight a disease it has not come into contact with before. Vaccines are designed to prevent disease, rather than treat a disease once you have caught it.

Vaccines are a simple, safe and effective way of protecting people against harmful diseases. Immunisation through vaccines is one of the best ways to protect you, your children and your community from the spread of preventable diseases.
