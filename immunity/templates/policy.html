Immunisation policies in Australian

No Jab, No Pay policy

Families with children who are not immunised according to the childhood vaccination Schedule appropriate for the child’s age (and do not have an approved exemption) will have a reduction applied to their Family Tax Benefit (FTB) Part A for each child who does not meet the immunisation requirements. Your payments may reduce by up to $28.84 per fortnight

No Jab, No Play policy

If you live in New South Wales, Queensland, Victoria, Western Australia or South Australia:

If you plan to enrol your child at childcare or kindergarten, you must provide the service with a current Immunisation History Statement from the Australian Immunisation Register.

If you live in the Australian Capital Territory, Tasmania, or the Northern Territory:

Your child does not need to be immunised to attend child care services. However, if an outbreak occurs, unimmunised children may be excluded from child care for a period of time.
